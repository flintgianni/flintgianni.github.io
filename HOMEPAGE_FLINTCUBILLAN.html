Apple TV+, “Blush” Is a Filipino Animator’s Love Letter to His Wife

It's truly inspiring to see Filipino artists and designers abroad finally reach their major breakthrough... and it's even better when they get to tell their personal stories. Blush, an animated short film animated by Joe Mateo, a Filipino seasoned animator and storyboard artist working in the United States, is one such example.

This 15-minute film is his first as a writer and filmmaker, and it is based on his own experiences. He came up with the idea while grieving the loss of his wife, Mary Ann, who died of breast cancer in 2017. Another inspiration is their shared experience of relocating to a new country.
